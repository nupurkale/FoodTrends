About the Project

Diet, nutrition and expenditure on food are issues of special interest among young adults. Tracking expenditure and food intake is necessary to set health goals and curb expenditure on food that is unnecessary and wasteful. Our project focuses on drawing patterns and finding out trends of eating habits of individuals in the age group of 23-28. This analysis concentrates on two parts:

(1) Eating habits and peculiar patterns of individuals.

(2) Expenditure of every individual on different types of food products.

We are working on data that we have collected over the period of one year. This data contains information on grocery expenditure of two houses. Interestingly, the two houses are distinct based on gender. One house consists of male members while the other consists of female members. Through this project we also look forward to getting insights into the difference in eating habits of female and male individuals.

Project Design

The purpose of this project is to create a visualization report of the expenditure of individuals on types of food items over the course of one year and to study the eating trends of different individuals. This report provides insights into the expenditure of every individual on food items based on pre-defined categories. Our secondary aim is to find distinguished/similar patterns in eating habits between females and males. This will help users compare the eating habits of the two genders and also get insights into differences between their dietary habits.

Project Overview

This tool visualizes the data that we have collected of two households. This data includes the spending of each individual from the two houses on daily grocery, milk products, bakery items and junk food. The report visually represents the expenditure of each person. This report also enables comparison between the spending of each person. We also have drawn insights on the type of food majorly eaten by each person.

We have three themes for our result visualization:

Individual Expense Trends

For expenditure trends we have founnd trends at an individual level based on spending in a group and distribution of spending of a person based on different categories of food

Expenditure Comparison

On group level, comparison between monthly expenditure of female and male and monthly comparison of expenditure of the male house and female house based on categories of food.

Annual Intake Trend

Trends in intake of different kinds of food articles. Understand the difference in the quantity of food consumed by a gender or a person and how it affects the kind of nutrients the person consumes.
